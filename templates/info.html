Sessions and Information

Processing

Our first session features the language Processing, an extension of Java with a flexible software sketchbook. Designed to be a challenging and and fun camp for students who have little to no experience in programming, students will code their own projects and games!

Dates: July 26 - July 30
Time: 9 am - 12 pm

Java

Designed to be a faster-paced camp for students who have completed Processing or have had previous coding background, students will code using the language Java. Using their code, students will be able to learn about and experiment with object-oriented coding through fun projects and games.

Dates: August 2 - August 6
Time: 9 am - 12 pm
